NUS Exchange Checklist Timeline

Within 2–3 weeks of receiving admission offer
Accept offer, upload photo for NUS student card and wait for photo approval email.
Wait for Registration Part One email and complete it (wait 2 working days before changing NUS ID password).
Apply for housing once portal opens (June/November).
Within 3 weeks after receiving acceptance offer
Apply for housing (if not done already).
Submit Student’s Pass application after receiving instructional email from NUS.
3 weeks before start of exchange semester
Check NUS email for Registration Part Two [REG8000] and Student’s Pass Offsite Enrolment [OSE9000], book slots.
Check NUS email for Course Registration details.
1 week before coming to NUS
Download uNivUS app and pay fees before attending Registration Part Two.
Complete all 3 steps online for Student’s Pass application and prepare documents for Offsite Enrolment.
Book slot for Respect & Consent workshop in EduRec.
Week 0 / Weeks 1–2
Participate in Course Registration (add/drop/swop courses).
Attend Registration Part Two to collect NUS student card.
Attend Offsite Enrolment session for student's pass formalities.
Complete Student Essentials course on Canvas.
Attend Respect & Consent workshop.
